About Our Initiative

ScotiaShield is part of the 2024-2025 IMI BIGDataAIHUB Competition – a prestigious event organized by the Institute for Management & Innovation (IMI) at the University of Toronto. This competition brings together leading academics, industry experts, and talented teams to develop innovative Big Data and AI solutions.

About Scotiabank

Founded in 1832, Scotiabank has grown to become one of Canada’s leading financial institutions with a robust global presence. With a commitment to innovation, customer service, and responsible banking, Scotiabank sets industry standards in risk management and AML compliance.

About the IMI BIGDataAIHUB Competition

The IMI BIGDataAIHUB Competition is an annual event that challenges participants to tackle real-world problems using Big Data and Artificial Intelligence. Chaired by Professor Kevin Yousie and led by IMI at the University of Toronto, this competition fosters collaboration between academia and industry, pushing the boundaries of innovative research and practical applications.

Our Approach with ScotiaShield

ScotiaShield leverages Scotiabank’s deep expertise in risk management and AML practices to build a state-of-the-art solution. Using advanced machine learning and unsupervised learning techniques, our platform identifies suspicious transactions and anomalous patterns, helping to secure the financial ecosystem and combat financial crime.

About IMI & Our Partners

The Institute for Management & Innovation (IMI) at the University of Toronto is renowned for its cutting-edge research and commitment to fostering innovation. Collaborating with industry leaders like Scotiabank, IMI creates an environment where groundbreaking ideas are transformed into practical, impactful solutions.
